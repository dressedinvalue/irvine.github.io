Hi, welcome to my web page, I hope you like it. Here is some information about my career which may interest you. For about 15 years I have been working in graphic design, web development, and videography. My parents served as living examples, always very encouraging in art and business. Looking back, it seems inevitable the two would culminate in such a career. Over the years I have been afforded to work with many small and large businesses, entrepreneurs, organizations, conferences, musicians, artists, filmmakers, and photographers. Some of my own photography has been published in books and marketing materials across the country. In 1996 I earned an art award from my seventh-grade art teacher. In 2004 I finished a degree in Graphic Design and promptly began working for the low wages that this kind of work is valued at. But I remember fondly my time spent in the bowels of a printshop with ink-stained tradesmen, where I acquired the skills which helped me later when signing deals with their sales reps. I have worked within freelance and pro bono freedoms and in the shadows of pointy-haired bosses. Since 2010 I work full-time for the American Printing House for the Blind in Louisville, Kentucky.
